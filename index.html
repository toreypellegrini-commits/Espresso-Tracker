Log Shot

Every variable. One screen.

Dose, yield, ratio, grind, shot time, pre-infusion, temperature, days off roast — log it all in seconds. The ratio calculates automatically. Days off roast too. Rate the shot, add tasting notes, and move on.

By Bag Insights

See the pattern. Find the shot.

Six charts per bag — grind progression, dial-in trajectory, calibration curve, ratio vs rating, shot time vs rating, and taste score by days off roast. Finally understand what's actually working.

Community Shots

You're not dialling in alone.

Every 4★+ shot logged with a grinder gets shared with the community — extraction params only, no tasting notes. Starting a new bag? Search by grinder and coffee to find a starting point from someone who's already been there.

"What started as a Google Doc became a spreadsheet, and eventually this."

About a month after getting my first proper espresso machine, I started tracking shots to improve my dial-in. The more I logged, the more I wondered whether something like this could benefit the broader espresso community.

Dialed is a free, community-built app for home baristas. There are no plans to monetize it. It's in active beta — bugs will happen, features will improve. If you have ideas, I want to hear them.
